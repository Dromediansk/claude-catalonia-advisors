# Aeroport Josep Tarradellas Barcelona–El Prat (BCN)

Two terminals: **T1** (most full-service airlines) and **T2** (low-cost; Vueling, Ryanair, easyJet — also where the Rodalies train station is). A **free shuttle bus** runs T1 ↔ T2 (every ~5–7 min, ~10 min ride) `[verify]`.

## Options at a glance

| Option | Time to centre | Price `[verify]` | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Metro L9 Sud** | ~32 min to Collblanc/Torrassa transfer | €5.90 one-way, OR free with Hola BCN card | Budget travellers staying off L1/L3 corridor |
| **Aerobús (A1/A2)** | ~35 min to Plaça de Catalunya | €7.45 one-way / €12.85 return | Luggage-heavy, want a direct ride to Catalunya |
| **Rodalies R2 Nord** | ~19 min to Sants, ~26 min to Pg de Gràcia | ~€4.90 single (Zone 1) | Cheapest. T2 only |
| **Taxi** | 20–35 min | ~€35–45 metered, min €21 from airport | Door-to-door, late night, family |
| **Cabify / Free Now** | 20–35 min | Often a few euros cheaper than taxi | App-based, English-friendly |

## Metro L9 Sud

- **Stations at airport**: Aeroport T1, Aeroport T2.
- **Fare**: ~€5.90 one-way single Airport Ticket `[verify]`. Standard T-Casual does **NOT** cover the airport stations.
- **Hola Barcelona Travel Card** covers L9 to/from airport — best deal if doing the trip + multi-day travel.
- **Key catch**: L9 Sud does **not** connect to most central metro lines directly. From the airport you must transfer:
  - **Torrassa** → L1 (red)
  - **Collblanc** → L5 (blue)
  - Then proceed to Catalunya / Sants / Sagrada Família etc.
- ~32 min airport → Collblanc/Torrassa `[verify]`.
- Trains every ~7 min `[verify]`.

## Aerobús (A1 and A2)

- **A1** = Terminal 1 ↔ Plaça de Catalunya
- **A2** = Terminal 2 ↔ Plaça de Catalunya
- Stops at **Pl. Espanya**, **Gran Via – Urgell**, **Pl. Universitat**, **Pl. Catalunya** (and reverse).
- **Frequency**: every 5–10 min `[verify]`
- **Time**: ~35 min `[verify]`
- **Fare** `[verify]`: ~€7.45 one-way / ~€12.85 return (90 days)
- **Luggage**: dedicated racks. Buggy/wheelchair friendly.
- **NOT covered by** T-Casual, T-Usual, T-Familiar, **or Hola Barcelona Card**. Buy from the machine at the stop or with contactless on board.

## Rodalies R2 Nord (Renfe)

- **From T2 only**. There is no train station at T1 — take the free shuttle from T1 to T2.
- Direct services to **Estació de Sants** (~19 min) and **Passeig de Gràcia** (~26 min), continuing to El Clot–Aragó, Sant Andreu, Granollers, Maçanet–Massanes `[verify]`.
- **Frequency**: every ~30 min `[verify]`.
- **Fare**: cheapest of all options, ~€4.90 single Zone 1 `[verify]`.
- Covered by **Hola Barcelona Travel Card** (Zone 1) — but L9 metro from T1 is more convenient if you have the card.

## Taxi

- **Yellow-and-black** Barcelona taxis are the only official ones.
- **Minimum fare from airport**: ~€21 `[verify]`.
- **Typical cost to centre**: €35–45 metered `[verify]`.
- Supplements may apply (luggage `[verify]`, T1/T2 pickup `[verify]`, night/weekend).
- **Fixed fare to Cruise Port (T-4)**: ~€45 `[verify]`.
- Pay by card universally accepted.
- Pick up at the official rank outside arrivals — never accept rides from touts inside the terminal.

## Cabify / Free Now / Bolt

- **Cabify** and **Bolt** operate VTC licensed cars at the airport — often a few euros cheaper than metered taxi.
- **Free Now** dispatches official yellow taxis through the app (no surge, metered fare).
- **Uber**: limited Barcelona presence; current regulations are phasing out VTC services in the metropolitan area — **check the app on arrival** `[verify current status]`.
- Designated pickup zones at both terminals (signposted "VTC").

## Night arrivals/departures

- **NitBus N17** (T1) and **N18** (T2): Pl. Catalunya ↔ Airport, ~60 min `[verify]`, every ~20 min from ~22:00 to ~05:00 `[verify]`. Cheapest after metro closes. Covered by TMB passes (but NOT Hola BCN for the airport segment — verify).
- Metro L9 Sud closes around midnight on weekdays; runs all-night Saturday `[verify]`.
- After Aerobús/metro stop: NitBus, taxi, or app.
