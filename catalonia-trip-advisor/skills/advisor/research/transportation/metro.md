# Barcelona Metro (TMB)

Operated by **Transports Metropolitans de Barcelona (TMB)**, except FGC lines.

## Lines

| Line | Colour | Operator | Key tourist stations |
|------|--------|----------|----------------------|
| L1   | Red    | TMB      | Estació de Sants, Plaça d'Espanya, Plaça de Catalunya, Arc de Triomf |
| L2   | Purple | TMB      | Sagrada Família, Passeig de Gràcia, Paral·lel |
| L3   | Green  | TMB      | Sants Estació, Espanya, Passeig de Gràcia, Catalunya, Liceu, Drassanes, Paral·lel, Vallcarca (for Park Güell), Lesseps |
| L4   | Yellow | TMB      | Barceloneta, Jaume I (Born/Gothic Quarter), Urquinaona, Passeig de Gràcia |
| L5   | Blue   | TMB      | Diagonal, Sagrada Família, Hospital Clínic |
| L6   | Lilac  | **FGC**  | Catalunya, Provença, Sarrià, Reina Elisenda |
| L7   | Brown  | **FGC**  | Catalunya, Gràcia, Av. Tibidabo |
| L8   | Pink   | **FGC**  | Espanya, Magòria, Molí Nou |
| L9 Nord / L9 Sud | Orange | TMB | **L9 Sud connects Aeroport T1 and T2** — separate fare (see `airport-transfers.md`) |
| L10 Nord / L10 Sud | Light blue | TMB | La Sagrera, Foneria, Zona Franca |
| L11 | Lime green | TMB    | Trinitat Nova ↔ Can Cuiàs (short shuttle) |

**FGC note**: L6/L7/L8 are run by Ferrocarrils de la Generalitat de Catalunya — fare integration with TMB passes in Zone 1, but operationally distinct. See `tram-and-fgc.md`.

## Operating hours

| Day | Hours `[verify]` |
|-----|------|
| Monday–Thursday | 05:00 – 24:00 |
| Friday & eve of public holiday | 05:00 – 02:00 |
| **Saturday** | **05:00 Saturday → continuous through Sunday morning (all-night)** |
| Sunday | runs through to 24:00 Sunday |
| Public holidays | 05:00 – 24:00 |

## Frequency

- Peak: every 2–4 min on busy lines (L1, L3, L5) `[verify]`
- Off-peak / late: 6–10 min `[verify]`
- Night service (Sat all-night): typically every 8–15 min `[verify]`

## Most useful lines for tourists

- **L3 (green)** — backbone for sightseeing: Sants → Espanya → Pg de Gràcia → Catalunya → Liceu → Drassanes
- **L2 (purple)** — Sagrada Família, Passeig de Gràcia
- **L4 (yellow)** — Barceloneta beach, Jaume I (Born/Gothic Quarter)
- **L5 (blue)** — Sagrada Família, Hospital Clínic

## Accessibility

Most stations have lifts, but a handful of older stations (especially on L3, L4) are still step-only. Check station accessibility in the **TMB app** before travel.

## Apps

- **TMB** (official) — routes, real-time, accessibility info
- **Citymapper** — best for tourists (combines metro, bus, FGC, tram)
- **Moovit** — solid alternative

## Tickets

**Never buy a single ticket.** A single (senzill) is ~€2.90 `[verify]` and is not worth it. Buy a **T-Casual** (10 trips, ~€13.00 `[verify]`) or **Hola Barcelona** card for tourists. See `tickets-passes.md`.

**Important**: standard metro tickets do NOT work for L9 Sud Aeroport T1/T2 stations — that requires either the Airport Ticket (~€5.90 `[verify]`) or the Hola Barcelona card.
