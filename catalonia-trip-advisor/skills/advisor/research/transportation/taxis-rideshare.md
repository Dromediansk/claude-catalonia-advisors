# Taxis and rideshare

## Official Barcelona taxis

- **Colour**: black-and-yellow only. Anything else at the airport or street is not an official Barcelona taxi.
- **Regulated by**: Institut Metropolità del Taxi (IMET), Àrea Metropolitana de Barcelona (AMB).
- **Meter**: mandatory for all rides except set fares (cruise port, etc.).
- **Roof light**: green = available, red/none = occupied or off-duty.

| Item | Approx. |

| Per-km tariff T-2 (nights/weekends) | ~€1.45/km |
| Luggage supplement (per piece >55×35×35cm) | ~€1 |
| Pickup at airport supplement | ~€4.50 |
| Pickup at Sants/Estació del Nord | ~€2.50 |
| Cruise port supplement | ~€4.50 |

Card payment is universally accepted; ask before boarding if uncertain.

## How to get one

- **Street hail**: green roof light = free.
- **Ranks**: airport, Sants, Pg de Gràcia, Pl. Catalunya, Pl. Espanya, all major sites.
- **Phone**: 932 222 222 (Radio Taxi) `[verify]`, 933 033 033 `[verify]`.
- **App**: **Free Now** — dispatches official metered taxis through the app, card payment in-app.

## Rideshare and VTC apps

| App | Status in Barcelona |
|---|---|
| **Free Now** | Official taxis only via the app. Universal, recommended. |
| **Cabify** | Active. VTC (private hire) cars, often a few € cheaper than metered taxi. Available at airport. |
| **Bolt** | Active (VTC). |
| **Uber** | Limited presence; new Catalan/metropolitan regulations are phasing out VTC ride-hailing in the Barcelona area by end of 2026. **Check the app on arrival** `[verify current status]`. |

**Regulatory context**: Catalan & metropolitan rules are progressively cutting VTC licences (~600 of 900 to expire by end of 2026 `[verify]`). Service availability may degrade — taxis remain the constant.

## Tipping

- **Not expected**.
- **Common**: round up to the nearest euro, or 5% for good service.

## Safety tips

- **Airport / Sants**: never accept rides from people approaching you inside the terminal. Walk to the marked rank outside.
- Confirm the meter is running at the start.
- Insist on a printed receipt (rebut/recibo) if you want one — drivers must provide it on request.
