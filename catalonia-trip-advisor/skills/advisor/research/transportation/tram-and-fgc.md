# Tram and FGC

## FGC (Ferrocarrils de la Generalitat de Catalunya)

Catalan-government-run rail, separate from TMB but **integrated with all standard passes in Zone 1** (T-Casual, T-Usual, T-Familiar, Hola Barcelona, T-jove).

### Inside Barcelona — Metro del Vallès / Línia de Balmes

Lines that **appear on the metro map** as L6/L7/L8 — these are FGC, not TMB:

| Line | Useful for tourists | Key stops |
|---|---|---|
| **L6** (lilac) | Sarrià, upscale uptown | Pl. Catalunya, Provença, Gràcia, Sarrià, Reina Elisenda |
| **L7** (brown) | **Tibidabo** (Av. Tibidabo → Tramvia Blau / bus → funicular → Tibidabo amusement park) | Pl. Catalunya, Provença, Gràcia, El Putxet, Av. Tibidabo |
| **L8** (pink) | Outer commuter (less tourist use) | Pl. Espanya, Magòria, Molí Nou |
| **L12** | Shuttle Sarrià ↔ Reina Elisenda |

### Suburban FGC lines

- **S1, S2** — to Terrassa
- **S5, S55** — to Sant Cugat / Universitat Autònoma
- **R5, R6** — to **Montserrat-Aeri** (cable car) and **Monistrol de Montserrat** (rack railway). Note: these are FGC's own "R" lines, **not** the same as Renfe Rodalies R5. Departs from **Pl. Espanya**. `[verify]`

### Stations

- **Plaça de Catalunya (FGC)** — different platform from the Renfe/Metro Catalunya; same building. Northbound lines start here.
- **Plaça d'Espanya (FGC)** — Montserrat lines & southbound suburban.
- **Provença** — connects to Diagonal metro (L3/L5).

## Tram (TRAM Barcelona)

Two separate networks run by **TRAM**, fully integrated with TMB/Hola Barcelona passes.

### Trambaix (T1, T2, T3)

- Western network. Starts at **Francesc Macià** (Diagonal/Pg. de Gràcia area).
- Heads through Diagonal, Zona Universitària, into Baix Llobregat (Esplugues, Sant Joan Despí, Sant Just Desvern, Sant Feliu de Llobregat).
- Lines mostly share track until they branch out.
- **Tourist use**: limited unless visiting Camp Nou area or western suburbs.

### Trambesòs (T4, T5, T6)

- Eastern network. Starts at **Glòries** (Diagonal/Pl. de les Glòries).
- Connects to Forum / Diagonal Mar (T4), Sant Adrià de Besòs (T4/T6), Badalona (T5).
- **Tourist use**: **T4** is useful for getting to the **Forum** area, beaches at Bogatell/Mar Bella, the **Disseny Hub Barcelona**, and DiagonalMar mall.

### Diagonal connection

The two networks **do not yet connect** through central Diagonal. Connection construction underway, expected complete **2027** `[verify]`.

### Hours & frequency `[verify]`

- ~05:00–24:00 weekdays
- Until ~02:00 Friday/Saturday
- Every 7–15 min depending on time
