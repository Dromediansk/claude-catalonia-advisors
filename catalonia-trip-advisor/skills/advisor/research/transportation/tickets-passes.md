# Which pass to buy — decision guide

All passes (unless noted) are integrated across **TMB metro, TMB bus, FGC Zone 1, Rodalies Zone 1, Tram**. Most do NOT cover the L9 Sud airport stops (Aeroport T1 / Aeroport T2).

## Quick decision

| Visiting for… | Buy |
|---|---|
| 2–3 days, light use, no airport metro | **T-Casual** |
| 2–5 days, airport via L9 + heavy use | **Hola Barcelona Travel Card** |
| 1+ month resident-style | **T-Usual** |
| Family/group, sharing trips | **T-Familiar** |
| Under 30, long stay | **T-jove** |

## Single ticket (Bitllet senzill)

- **Price**: ~€2.90 `[verify]` (rose from €2.65 in 2025)
- **Valid for**: one journey, no transfers between modes
- **Verdict**: Not worth it. Always buy a multi-trip card.

## T-Casual

- **Who**: Short-stay tourist, light/medium use, **not** using airport metro.
- **What**: 10 single journeys, **one person only** (non-transferable), each journey up to 1h 15min with transfers.
- **Zone 1** covers all of Barcelona city and metropolitan area (incl. airport bus stop, but **not** L9 Aeroport stations).
- **Price**: ~€13.00 (Zone 1) `[verify]`. Zone 2 ~€25.50 `[verify]`.

## T-Usual

- **Who**: Long stay (over ~30 days) or someone using public transport heavily for a month.
- **What**: 30-day **unlimited** travel, single person (non-transferable).
- **Price**: ~€22.80 (Zone 1) `[verify]` — significantly discounted under current Catalan subsidy.
- **Covers L9 airport stops**: yes `[verify]`.

## T-Familiar

- **Who**: Two or more travellers sharing trips.
- **What**: 8 journeys within **30 consecutive days**, shareable between any number of users (multiple validations allowed in same trip).
- **Price**: ~€11.05 `[verify]`.
- Each journey ≤ 75 min duration.

## Hola Barcelona Travel Card

- **Who**: Tourist doing the **airport on L9** + lots of trips per day.
- **What**: **Unlimited** travel on TMB metro (incl. L9 Sud Aeroport T1/T2), TMB bus, Tram, FGC Zone 1, Rodalies Renfe Zone 1. Valid 2 / 3 / 4 / 5 **consecutive** days (24-hour blocks from first validation).
- **Prices** `[verify]`:
  - 2 days: ~€18.70
  - 3 days: ~€27.30
  - 4 days: ~€35.60
  - 5 days: ~€43.60
- **10% online discount** if bought via holabarcelona.com — pick up at machines using the QR.
- **Best buy** for 3+ day tourists using the airport metro.

## T-jove

- **Who**: Under 30 (must be registered/personalised).
- **What**: 90 days unlimited travel.
- **Price**: ~€44–45.50 (Zone 1) `[verify]` — current discounted rate.
- Requires a personal **T-mobilitat** card with ID.

## Targeta Rosa

- **Who**: Barcelona residents over 60 or with reduced mobility (low income).
- **Not for tourists** — requires residency registration.

## Airport ticket (Bitllet Aeroport)

- **What**: Single ride on L9 Sud to/from Aeroport T1 or T2.
- **Price**: ~€5.90 one-way `[verify]`. Return separate.
- Use this only if you are NOT buying Hola Barcelona and need L9 to/from the airport. T-Casual does **not** cover the airport L9 stops.

## Where to buy

- Vending machines at every metro/FGC station (cards + cash; English available).
- TMB and T-mobilitat apps.
- **Hola Barcelona**: holabarcelona.com (10% online discount) → exchange QR at airport/metro machines.
