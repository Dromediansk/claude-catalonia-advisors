# Sample Itineraries

Plans are clustered by neighbourhood to minimise transit. Each day lists a morning anchor, lunch area, afternoon, and evening. "If it rains" notes are included for weather contingencies. Bookings flagged as **[BOOK]** should be reserved before arrival.

## 3-Day Barcelona Essentials

### Day 1 — Eixample + Gòtic
- Morning: **[BOOK]** Sagrada Família, first slot ~09:00. Walk Avinguda de Gaudí toward Hospital de Sant Pau (optional add-on tour).
- Lunch: Eixample around Passeig de Sant Joan or Mercat de la Concepció.
- Afternoon: walk down Passeig de Gràcia, exterior view of **[BOOK]** Casa Batlló and La Pedrera (do one of them — Casa Batlló more colourful, La Pedrera more architectural). Continue south to Plaça de Catalunya.
- Evening: stroll into Barri Gòtic via Portal de l'Àngel. Catedral de Barcelona, Plaça del Rei, Plaça Reial. Tapas dinner in El Born (Cal Pep, Bar del Pla — book or arrive at 19:30 sharp).
- If it rains: Sagrada Família + La Pedrera attic exhibits are sheltered; swap walking for Casa Batlló's full interior tour.

### Day 2 — Park Güell + Gràcia + Bunkers
- Morning: **[BOOK]** Park Güell Monumental Zone, first slot. Walk down through the free park.
- Lunch: Gràcia — Plaça de la Vila de Gràcia or Carrer Verdi. Try Con Gracia or Cervecería Catalana (no reservations, expect a queue).
- Afternoon: wander Gràcia plaças — Plaça del Sol, Plaça del Diamant. Coffee at SlowMov.
- Evening: bus 119 or taxi up to Bunkers del Carmel for sunset (note: area closed by police around sunset in 2026 — arrive 90 min before for the view, leave before closing) [verify]. Dinner back in Gràcia.
- If it rains: skip Bunkers (no shelter); replace with CosmoCaixa (L7 FGC Av. Tibidabo) or Hospital de Sant Pau guided tour.

### Day 3 — El Born + Barceloneta + Montjuïc
- Morning: **[BOOK]** Museu Picasso first slot. Santa Maria del Mar afterwards (free general entry).
- Lunch: Barceloneta seafood — Can Solé or La Cova Fumada (cash, no reservations, classic). Or Passeig Joan de Borbó for terraces.
- Afternoon: walk Barceloneta beach + boardwalk. Take metro L4 to Espanya, ride up to Montjuïc. **[BOOK]** MNAC (or visit Saturday from 15:00 for free with reservation).
- Evening: Magic Fountain show at Plaça d'Espanya (Thu–Sun seasonal schedule — verify before going) [verify]. Dinner Poble-sec — Carrer de Blai pintxos crawl.
- If it rains: replace Barceloneta walk with MNAC + Fundació Miró back-to-back. Magic Fountain runs in light rain but cancels in storms.

## 5-Day Barcelona Deeper

Days 1–3 as above. Then:

### Day 4 — Modernisme Deep-Dive + Sant Antoni
- Morning: **[BOOK]** Palau de la Música Catalana guided tour (~50 min).
- Late morning: **[BOOK]** Hospital de Sant Pau — walk between the two via Carrer de la Princesa and the Eixample grid (or metro L1 Urquinaona → L5 Sant Pau | Dos de Maig).
- Lunch: Sant Antoni — Tickets/Bodega 1900 area (book ahead) or simpler at Mercat de Sant Antoni stalls.
- Afternoon: Sunday only — Mercat de Sant Antoni book and coin market. Otherwise: Casa Vicens (Gaudí's first house, metro L3 Fontana) — quieter than Casa Batlló.
- Evening: Vermouth crawl on Carrer del Parlament; dinner in Poble-sec.
- If it rains: doubles down on Palau + Sant Pau (both indoor).

### Day 5 — Day trip OR slow Poblenou
- **Option A — Montserrat half-day** then return for Poblenou afternoon.
- **Option B — Poblenou + beach**: morning at Mercat dels Encants (flea market, Thu/Fri/Sat/Sun mornings [verify]) and Torre Glòries area, lunch on Rambla del Poblenou, afternoon at Bogatell beach, evening drinks at Espai Joliu or Balius.
- If it rains: Disseny Hub Barcelona (design museum at Glòries), then CCCB or MACBA in El Raval.

## 7-Day Barcelona + 1–2 Day Trips

Days 1–5 as above (5-Day Deeper).

### Day 6 — Day trip: pick one
- **Girona** (~38 min by AVE) — full day in old town + Jewish quarter.
- **Montserrat** (~1h15) — if not already done in Day 5.
- **Sitges** (~35 min) — if you want a beach + village day with minimal effort.

### Day 7 — Day trip: contrast with Day 6
- **Tarragona** (~32 min by Avant) — Roman ruins + seafront.
- **Penedès** (~45 min) — cava cellar tour, half-day; afternoon back in BCN for shopping or a last-minute museum.
- **Figueres + Dalí** (~55 min by AVE) — pair with Castell de Sant Ferran.
- If both day-trip days rain: split into one half-day Girona morning + one indoor BCN afternoon (Fundació Miró + MACBA).

## 7-Day Catalonia Loop (Barcelona + Girona + Costa Brava)

**Logistics**: 3 nights Barcelona, 1 night Girona, 2 nights Costa Brava (Begur or Cadaqués base), then 1 last night Barcelona. A rental car picked up in Girona or Figueres makes the coast workable; without one, stick to Cadaqués + town walks.

### Day 1 — Barcelona Eixample + Gòtic
As Day 1 of Essentials above.

### Day 2 — Park Güell + Gràcia
As Day 2 of Essentials above.

### Day 3 — Born + Barceloneta + Montjuïc
As Day 3 of Essentials above.

### Day 4 — Train to Girona, settle, evening explore
- Morning: AVE Sants → Girona (~38 min). Drop bags at hotel near Plaça de la Independència or in the old town.
- Lunch: La Fàbrica or Federal Café in the old town.
- Afternoon: walk the city walls (Passeig de la Muralla), Catedral de Girona, El Call (Jewish quarter), Banys Àrabs.
- Evening: dinner — book Divinum or Boira on the riverfront for the Onyar-houses view.
- If it rains: cathedral + Museu d'Història dels Jueus + Cinema Truffaut.

### Day 5 — Pick up car at Figueres, Dalí, drive to coast
- Morning: drive or train (R11) to Figueres. **[BOOK]** Teatre-Museu Dalí (~2h inside).
- Lunch: Figueres centre.
- Afternoon: drive to Cadaqués (~1h) via the GI-614 coastal road. Settle in.
- Evening: walk to Portlligat (Dalí's house — **[BOOK]** for next morning), dinner overlooking the bay at Compartir or Casa Anita.
- If it rains: Castell de Sant Ferran (Figueres) is partly covered; Dalí museum is fully indoor.

### Day 6 — Cap de Creus + Costa Brava beaches
- Morning: **[BOOK]** Casa-Museu Dalí Portlligat (timed small-group entry).
- Late morning: drive to Cap de Creus lighthouse, walk part of the headland.
- Lunch: Cadaqués or El Port de la Selva.
- Afternoon: south along coast — coves around Roses or down to L'Escala / L'Estartit. Stop at Empúries Greek/Roman ruins.
- Evening: dinner in Begur or Calella de Palafrugell.
- If it rains: Empúries archaeological site has covered exhibits; Dalí museum bonus visit.

### Day 7 — Coastal villages, return to Barcelona
- Morning: coastal walk — Camí de Ronda from Calella de Palafrugell to Llafranc (~1h), or Begur calas (Sa Tuna, Sa Riera).
- Lunch: Calella de Palafrugell or Tamariu.
- Afternoon: drive back, drop the car in Girona or Barcelona depending on contract.
- Evening: last dinner in Barcelona — El Born or Poble-sec.
- If it rains: skip the coastal walk, drive straight back; spend afternoon in Barcelona at any sight saved from earlier.

## Itinerary Notes
- Always check official sites the morning of for closures (especially Mondays for many museums).
- Major museums often have free first Sunday of the month — booking opens 4 days ahead at 09:00 Barcelona time and disappears fast.
- T-Casual ticket (10 metro rides) is the right pass for short stays; T-Familiar exists for groups but check current zones.
- Sundays: most non-tourist shops shut; many small restaurants closed Sunday night + all day Monday.
- Mid-August: book everything well in advance and expect 30%+ of small businesses on holiday.
